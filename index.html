About the night

A night to pour out
before the Lord

There is a place at the altar where words fail and worship takes over — a holy exchange between a weary soul and a faithful God. Finally is an evening of deep, intimate worship where we lay down our burdens, open our hearts, and encounter the presence of the Lord together.

Rooted in Psalm 142:2, this gathering invites you to come as you are — not to perform, but to pour out. To let your worship rise as incense before the throne.

"I pour out my complaint before him; I tell my trouble before him."
